What is OpenTee?

OpenTee is a tee time marketplace built for Austin golfers.

Every tee time listed connects directly to the course's tee sheet. What you see is what's actually available — not scraped, not estimated, not stale.

Find a time, compare courses, book in under 30 seconds. Courses across Austin in one place, with real photos, current conditions, and weather built in so you know what you're walking into.

Every booking earns rewards — across every course, every round. Challenges give you a reason to explore somewhere new. It all compounds over time.

And finally, a way to book beyond the standard foursome without calling the pro shop.
